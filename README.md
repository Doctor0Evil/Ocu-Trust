# Ocu‑Trust

Ocu‑Trust is a biophysical oculus‑system for real‑world, eco‑aligned visual computing. It focuses on oculus‑gaming and organic‑computing experiences that feel like “the real thing,” while using biodegradable, host‑safe, and environmentally safe designs with intentionally limited lifetimes. [nature](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41528-024-00360-w)

Ocu‑Trust is designed for augmented citizens, cybernetic stacks, and labs that want ocular interfaces and controllers to be **organically compatible**, neurorights‑aligned, and materially sustainable. [en.wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Biodegradable_electronics)

## Sustainability and Safety Notes

- “Biodegradable” is treated as a **scientific claim**, backed by materials that degrade to non‑toxic products under realistic conditions, not just fragmentation into microplastics. [news.northeastern](https://news.northeastern.edu/2025/12/19/degradability-transient-electronics-research/)
- All device designs are evaluated both for **host biocompatibility** (ocular safety, systemic exposure) and **ecosystem impact** (soil/water degradation, plant/animal safety) before being accepted into the Ocu‑Trust catalog. [pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC8022537/)

Ocu‑Trust’s goal is to make advanced oculus‑gaming and organic computing possible **without sacrificing the biological host or the planet**—every pixel and packet stays inside explicitly defined, evidence‑anchored safety and sustainability envelopes. [peakermap](https://www.peakermap.com/blogs/news/designing-sustainable-vr-ar-tech-and-impact)
